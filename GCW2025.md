# PRIME-DRE GCW 2025 
We are proud to announce the 2025 **PRIMatE Data and Resource Exchange (PRIME-DRE) Global Collaboration Workshop (GCW)**. This year’s workshop will again take place online on December 4-5, 2025 (9am-12pm EST / 3pm-6pm CET / 9pm-12am UTC+7). 

This year’s GCW will again bring together researchers and data scientists from across the PRIME-DRE network to share insights, explore new methodologies, and co-develop opportunities for collaboration. Whether you're working on neuroimaging, behavioral, electrophysiology or metadata, we look forward to hearing your voice in the conversation.

Here's an outline of the program:

## Day 1 (Dec 4) Community Goals    
- Introduction/reminder of PRIME-DRE Initiatives & Transition    
- Works-in-progress I (Flash talks)    
- Invited talks    
- Looking ahead: potential special issue submissions
- Virtual reception

## Day 2 (Dec 5) Generative Discussions     
- Works-in-progress II (Flash talks)    
- Brain-hack
- Closing remarks     
- Virtual reception

More general info will follow through this website and the mailing list. Specific info will be distributed to registrants through their the email addresses provided at registration.     

# Works-in-progress (Flash talks)     
Both days will feature a flash talk session in which PRIME-DRE consortium members of all career stages will briefly present themselves and their scientific interests to the community.
